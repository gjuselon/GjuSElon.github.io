Description

Who is Elon Musk?
He is the founder, CEO and Chief Engineer at SpaceX; early-stage investor, CEO and Product Architect of Tesla, Inc.; founder of The Boring Company; and co-founder of Neuralink and OpenAI. With an estimated net worth of around US$255 billion as of December 2021, Musk is the wealthiest person in the world.
How Elon Musk started his career?
Born and raised in South Africa, Musk spent time in Canada before finally moving to the U.S. Educated at the University of Pennsylvania in physics, Musk started getting his feet wet as a serial tech entrepreneur with early successes like Zip2 and X.com. He was instrumental in creating the company that became PayPal.
